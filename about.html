HSE PLAN

ASETVO HSE plan covers all aspects of community Affairs , Safety, Health , Environment and Security (CASHES) Practice and applicable to the proposed work on site. It highlights the companys corporate CASHES policy, regulations , work procedure and associated hazard as well as precautions to handle them .

It also evaluates some emergency cases and describes standard responsive actions a worker can take to tackle such emergencies. This document guides the execution of our projects. Incorporated in this CASHES plan is a detailed HSE Plan which clearly depicts our HSE values. This document is reviewed every year by Astevo HSE Department and learning points from our past operations are incorporated within.
