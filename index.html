ASTEVO NIGERIA LIMITED

ASTEVO NIGERIA LIMITED

is an indigenous oil and gas Services outfit, set up to render high quality Services to the oil and gas industry, and allied companies. The company was incorporated under the laws of the federal republic of Nigeria in 1991, Astevo has maintained a corporate goal of offering and sustaining a professional excellence service, alloyed with sound business practices in all its area of operation. Astevo have been involved in Instrumentation and control, pipeline and flow line construction, fabrication and structural works, Electrical construction services in the Oil & Gas industry. Whenever and wherever our services are required, we supply the best experienced skilled and unskilled labour. Guaranteeing client satisfaction as the end result, Astevo Nigeria Ltd is committed in providing timely, innovative and cost effective services to meet client needs at all times. Quality and safety is never compromised in our operations.
